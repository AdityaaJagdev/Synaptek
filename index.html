Privacy Policy

Synaptek Infotech ERP App

Company: Synaptek Infotech

At Synaptek Infotech, we are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of every user — be it students, teachers, or administrators. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, use, protect, and share the information when you use our ERP application.

We request all users to read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

🔹 1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our ERP Application – available for Student, Teacher, and Admin modules. It governs the data collected through the app’s features, including:

  • Syllabus Management
  • Paper Generation
  • Time Table
  • Exam & Results
  • Chatbox & Notifications
  • User Profiles & Academic Records

🔹 2. Information We Collect

We only collect data that is essential for functionality, security, and service improvement.

A. Personal Data

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • User Role (Student, Teacher, Admin)
  • Profile Picture (optional)

B. Academic & Institutional Data

  • Class, Subject, Section
  • Attendance Records
  • Marks & Grades
  • Assigned Syllabus and Lesson Plans
  • Homework & Exam Data

C. App Usage Data

  • App version, Device Type
  • Login Timestamp
  • In-app activity (for analytics and bug fixes)

D. Communication Data

  • Chat history within the app
  • Notification logs
  • Feedback or queries submitted

🔹 3. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow secure login and role-based access
  • To manage academic schedules, syllabus, and assessments
  • To generate and manage exam papers and results
  • To enable seamless communication between students, teachers, and admins
  • To send real-time alerts, updates, and academic notifications
  • To monitor app performance and user satisfaction
  • To ensure safety, compliance, and prevent abuse

🔹 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • We never sell or trade your data to third parties.
  • Information is shared only with authorized school staff or admin for institutional management.
  • Data may be shared with legal authorities under applicable laws or court orders.
  • Service providers helping us run the app are contractually bound to protect your data.

🔹 5. Data Security & Storage

  • All data encrypted with industry-standard protocols (e.g., AES-256)
  • Communication secured via SSL
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data stored in secure cloud with daily backups
  • Role-based access to ensure data confidentiality

🔹 6. User Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request corrections of inaccurate info
  • Request deletion of data (school approval needed)
  • Disable notifications or delete chat history
  • Withdraw consent or restrict data processing

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

🔹 7. Children’s Privacy

The ERP App is intended for institutional use. Children under the age of 13 must use the app under the supervision of a teacher or guardian. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without proper consent from school authorities.

🔹 8. Data Retention

  • Data retained for academic and operational needs
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations
  • To resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Data is permanently deleted when no longer needed.

🔹 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this policy at any time
  • Major changes will be notified through the app
  • Updated version will display “Last Updated” date
  • Using the app after changes means acceptance
